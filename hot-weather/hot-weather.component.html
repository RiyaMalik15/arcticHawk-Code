Summers


Summer or pre-monsoon season, lasting from April to June (April to July in northwestern India). In western and southern regions, the hottest month is April; for northern regions of India, May is the hottest month.

What is done in Summer season?

Mulching: Mulching is covering of soil to prevent moisture loss. And it is more important to cover the soil during summer, as temperature get high and increases evapotranspiration resulting in dry soil with cracks.

Generally , soil can be covered by any substance that prevents moisture loss. But,to reduce costs,any plant materials from such as straw from rice can be used. Also plants can be used as live mulch.

Mulching has lot of benefits like

  • Reducing moisture loss.
  • Reduce soil temperature.
  • Supressing the weed growth.
  • Improve fertility of soil.
  • Reduces soil erosion.

Crops such as cowpea also increase nitrogen content when used as mulch crop.
